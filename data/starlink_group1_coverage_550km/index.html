Starlink Group 1 Coverage

Pinned Footprints

In cursor-linked mode, double-click on the map to pin the current footprint. Pinned footprints keep their own color and label so you can compare likely shell spacing or regional overlap by eye.

Assumptions

Altitude: 550 km above the reference Earth radius.
Elevation mask: 25° minimum line-of-sight elevation.
Earth radius: 6378.137 km.
Map data: OpenStreetMap raster tiles via Leaflet.
